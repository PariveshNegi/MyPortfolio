Lecture 8: Personal Interview

In this lecture, we discussed how to prepare for a personal interview. We covered the importance of understanding the company, researching the interviewer, and practicing common interview questions. We also discussed how to dress professionally and how to make a good first impression.

Tips to prepare for a Personal Interview :

  • Research the company and the position you're applying for.
